## 1. The deep reading — why the course works
- Master ladder: willpower → habits → algorithms (move behavior from expensive-conscious to free-automatic). 300 days = states→traits repetition count (Wilber).
- Deepest tension: ASPIRATION vs ACCEPTANCE ("Paradox of Optimizing", VI.7). Johnson's resolution: Optimus is not a destination — already "just out of frame"; the demon is a faulty algorithm, never your identity; Mis-Take reframe; joy = the evidence you're doing it right.
- Push vs recovery → hormesis/golden mean; obstacles-become-content (antifragile alchemy); recovery is a design choice, not a failure state.
- Universal vs idiosyncratic → the U-loop: universal law + personal Prescription (#1 Self-Care / #1 Start / #1 Stop).
- Pedagogy: theory-before-practice (transfer); #1-Practice flagging (minimum effective dose); spaced repetition of ~12 big ideas per module; celebration as neurochemistry (dopamine timing), not niceness; dependency-ordered modules.

ALTER agrees on the diagnosis (PUSH vs WAY-OUT) and holds MORE radical resolutions: catch-is-the-unit, recovery-speed metric, graduation endgame.

## 2. Software's three unfair advantages over the course (the "why we haven't yet" answer)
1. **Teach by MOMENT, not calendar** — the course schedules lessons; the app waits for life to produce the teachable moment (obstacle logged → Hero Sandwich; temptation → +3 Catalyst; pre-sleep → Tranquility algorithm).
2. **Dose at RUNTIME** — the course ships 4 meditation lengths and hopes; the Sequencer already computes floor/standard/ceiling per bead per open.
3. **Pre-fill with LIVED EVIDENCE** — the course asks you to journal your data; the app HAS the data. Worksheets stop being imagination exercises and become mirrors ("Prior Best" + "shine a flashlight", fused).

## 3. The guardian question — options
- **G1 · Guardian (as-is):** separate winged witness. Soul-locked qualities (witness-not-coach, reward-never-shame, initiates-never-coerces) all fit. Safe.
- **G2 · Optimus declared:** the being IS your best self, said out loud in onboarding. Course-native, but David already rejected "вторая версия тебя" as cheesy, and a best-self *watching you drift* is a shame vector (violates reward-never-shame on bad days).

Flourishing = your guardian thriving. Late-chapter line: «Ты уже знаешь, кто я». Never says "best self"; shows it. The mint card's "matte now — ignites as you live" was already pointing here.

## 4. The nervous system (how the four organs work in tandem)
- **ONBOARDING writes the PROFILE:** dose ceiling, register (voice), year-words (identity vocabulary), good-day ingredients, blocker shape, pact. All GLANCE-dose chips.
- **THE SEQUENCER (built, unwired) braids each day:** acute override → bookend baton → in-context course bead → goal step → chain-at-risk → celebrate seal; capped by appetite nodeCap 0–3.
- **THE JOURNEY renders the braid as the world;** chapters = course dependency order; every node unlock = a MECHANIC in the planner (capability, not consumption).
