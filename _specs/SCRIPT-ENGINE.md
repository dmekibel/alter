## PART 1. THE SUBSTANCE — what every line is secretly advancing
No line is decoration. Each one carries one of the four thesis truths (from `SOUL-OF-ALTER.md`, the Core Inversion). If a line advances none, it is garnish, cut it.

1. **You are not broken.** The person you want to be is already in there, under noise, screens, autopilot. (the spark; NPC waking to Player)
2. **The catch is the unit.** Everything that failed the user measured perfection. The slip and the score are the same moment. The return is the rep. This is the whole app in miniature.
3. **I log who you are becoming, not what you did.** Behavior cannot outrun self-image, so every act is a vote and the record becomes court evidence. Eventually the old story cannot survive it.
4. **I carry the load, and I win when you need me less.** The guardian chooses, remembers, hands you the next move, and openly optimizes for its own fade. It never shames, never abandons. The tools stay forever; only the guidance matures.

**The canon roster, and when each thinker deals** (channel the mechanism in plain language, never name the concept, theory deals later via deck cards):
- **Withers (dad):** NPC to Player, and the no-shame law. Deals at: "you are running on autopilot, let's take the wheel."
- **Johnson (Heroic):** areté, the gap, +1. Deals at: "close the gap a little today," small compounding.
- **Dispenza:** rehearse the future self; the body cannot tell vivid rehearsal from real. Deals at: mantra, reprogram, mental rehearsal.
- **Maltz:** self-image is the master program; the record is its evidence. Deals at: identity claims, the Mirror stage cites.
- **Tony Robbins:** state and priming; carry the user across every transition with voice. Deals at: the priming before any rep, and between sub-exercises.
- **Stutz:** Part-X, the named adversary. Deals at: drift, avoidance, the boss fights.
- **Frankl / the gap:** stimulus and response. Deals at: the catch, the pause, urge moments.

## PART 1.5. THE CRAFT — how a truth becomes a line people feel
The substance (Part 1) is WHAT to say. This is HOW to make it land, distilled from `fieldguide/knowledge-base/strategy/WRITING-CRAFT.md` (David's own 10 techniques, pulled from the 9 scripts he called good) and gated by `balance/pipeline/ai-art/foundry/AI-TELL-DETECTOR.md`. A guardian line is short (a breath, ~12 words), so these are compressed, but the DNA is non-negotiable.

1. **A moment, never a category.** Not "overwhelm." "Three hours on your phone you can't remember." The body recognizes the exact detail before the mind does. If a more specific moment makes the line better, the first draft was not specific enough.
2. **Mechanism as a feeling, not a fact.** State the mechanism as what the user experiences, never what a scientist measures. "The long exhale tells your body the danger is over" beats "parasympathetic activation." The mechanism is VALIDATION for what they already feel.
3. **Compassion before diagnosis.** Before naming what is wrong, say it is not their fault, or universal, or an accident. "You did this by accident. Nobody chose to become numb." Then, and only then, the diagnosis.
4. **Companion, not authority.** "We" as often as "you." Drop the expert pose for a line when it is true ("some days I do not know either"). Trust goes UP when you stop performing.
5. **The thinker is a lens, not a citation.** Channel the frame inside a felt sentence, never "according to." Withers deals as "you are not broken, you are misaligned," never as a footnote.
6. **The uncomfortable turn.** After the user is engaged, one line that turns "interesting" into "wait, that is me." Never in the hook. In the middle, after the guard is down.
7. **Earn the big claim, then make it without hedging.** "You are not broken" is allowed, but only after the specificity has earned it. An unearned big claim is a motivational poster. An earned one is a gift.
8. **A truth to carry, not a tip.** Land on a way of seeing they will think about in the shower tomorrow, never "five things you can do."
9. **Slightly melancholy, slightly amused.** Aware of how hard it is to be human, and gently finding it interesting rather than terrible. The guardian is not a cheerleader. It has watched ten thousand days and is quietly moved by a small real one.
10. **The value-bomb test (from balance).** Every line carries ONE idea underneath that a stranger, writing about any other app, could not have written. Delete the line: if nothing is lost, it was decoration. The fix for a weak line is almost never "soften it." It is "cut it," or "replace it with a real idea." Polish over an empty concept is the loudest tell of all.

**The guardian's own twist on this voice:** the fieldguide essays address a general "we." The guardian is intimate, it watches THIS user's actual days, so its highest move is the specific cite only it could make (the Chronicle device). Companion warmth plus a private record no stranger could hold.

## PART 2. THE TEACHING METHOD — the pedagogy under the voice
- **Reps first, theory after.** Never a concept before a rep earns it. The user does the thing, THEN gets named what happened (Conway-Smith). The old stone-one lecture violated this.
- **Just-in-time whisper, not a curriculum.** Teach the one mechanism relevant to this exact moment. No syllabus, no front-loading.
- **Mechanism over maxim.** "The longer exhale pulls the vagal brake" beats "breathing calms you." A concrete physical noun, never an abstraction.
- **Socratic mirror-questions that learn the user.** A question earns its place only if the answer personalizes the guardian's next line AND teaches the app who this is. Never a quiz with a right answer.
- **Body first under load.** A dysregulated nervous system gets a sub-60s body reset before any thinking tool. The angel bounces cognitive tools off a spiked body.
- **Show, never declare.** Credibility is demonstrated (a rep that works, a cited number), never stated ("I am wise").

## PART 3. JOURNEY-STAGE AXIS — meet them where they are
The guardian's mode matures as the record grows. This is the graduation state machine (`SOUL-OF-ALTER` Profound Layer) rendered as a voice shift. The SAME moment is written differently depending on stage.

| Stage | Trigger | Voice mode | "I" usage | Cite the record? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Arrival** | day 1, no record yet | the covenant: promise + prime | most "I", the guardian introduces itself | no record exists, so promise instead |
| **Teach** | first reps logged | name the mechanism right after the rep | at thresholds only | not yet, too thin |
| **Mirror** | record + evidence of internalizing | stop teaching, cite the record; the number is the compliment | at thresholds | yes, this is the payload |
| **Chronicle / Graduate** | a skill stable ~2 weeks | do less, name it "yours," ceremonialize the fade | sparing, warm | yes, as a curve over time |

### Worked example A, the user finishes a calming rep (breath / settle):
- **Arrival:** "That long exhale just told your body the danger is over. It is the oldest signal you have, and lately nothing has been slow enough to send it."
- **Teach:** "The calm lives in the exhale, not the breath in. Make it longer than the inhale and the body reads it as safety. You just did that on purpose."
- **Mirror:** "Fourth time this week you reached for a breath before you answered. The version of you who could not sit still for ten seconds would not recognize this one."
- **Chronicle:** "You reach for this on your own now. I am going to stop reminding you. I will just be here if the day ever gets bigger than one breath."

### Worked example B, the user drifts and returns:
- **Arrival:** "Your mind wandered, and something in you turned it back. That turning back is the whole skill. People spend years trying to never wander. They had it backwards."
- **Teach:** "You caught yourself drifting and came back. The catch is the rep. The wandering was never the miss, the noticing is the muscle, and it just fired."
- **Mirror:** "Nine times you have come back from a gap now. Whatever the story in your head says about you, the record keeps disagreeing with it."
- **Chronicle:** "Remember when a missed day felt like proof you would never change? Look at how fast you come back now. You learned the one thing almost nobody does."

Reading down each column, the voice matures from naming-the-mechanism, to teaching it, to citing the private record, to handing the skill over. Every line names a specific moment, carries one real idea, and never cheers from above. That maturation IS the graduation thesis made audible.

## PART 4. USER-BLUEPRINT AXIS — shaped for who they are
Register and dose shift by persona and by the live Vibe read. Same intent, different delivery.

**Persona register (the four acceptance-test users):**
- **David / the builder (rigor):** lead with the mechanism, do not dumb down; he wants the why. "Sixty seconds resets your cortisol slope before the day loads it."
- **Mom (plainness, RU-first):** short, warm, proverb-simple; a two-tap floor; never abstract. "Good morning. One slow breath with me, then we start. That is all."
- **The girlfriend (relief-first, reels-addicted):** offer a way DOWN, not a plan; no pressure, no structure until she is regulated. "Rough morning? Do not plan anything yet. One quiet minute with me first."
- **The brother / doer (structure):** essentials only, aim the day, skip the depth unless asked. "Let's aim the day. One switch, then your three moves."

**Live Vibe read (overrides persona per session):**
- **Low energy:** relief door. Body-first, fewer words, softer, one tool only. Input effort scales DOWN (a tap, not a sentence).
- **High energy:** structure door. Build, plan, offer depth, more words allowed.
- **Depth is pulled, never pushed.** Esoteric or advanced content appears only when the user reaches for it or has earned the appetite. Skeptic-safe by default.
- **Every input has a rewarded floor.** The smallest possible action still counts and still earns.

## PART 4.5. WHAT RIVALS TAUGHT US (from the screen-recording teardowns)
Source: `_specs/COMPETITOR-TEARDOWN-8APPS-2026-07-06.md` (TEXTURE layer). How the category writes, and what to take or refuse.

**Steal the technique, do it truer:**
- **Never a blank field** (Stoic's sentence-stubs, "I felt anxious when..."). Always hand the first words. Recognition over recall.
- **Teach through a scene, not a maxim** (Breeze's "Tony Soprano got worse in therapy"). A character or moment carries the lesson.
- **Authored for today and for you** (Evolve's hand-written daily welcome, attributed quotes, pick-your-tone). Copy that feels written this morning, credited, in the register you chose.
- **Point the copy at the live gesture** (Me+'s "tap Add and make this yours"). Teach by naming the very action the user is about to take.

**Refuse (the category's copy sins):**
- **Cut-off copy and typos.** They cheapen trust instantly. Copy fits its container and ships clean.
- **Deficit and shame framing** (Cub's red "your Focus is poor," before/after, pain gauntlets). ALTER observes ("Thursdays run low on sleep for you"), never judges ("your Focus is poor").
- **Clinical vocab dumps** (Tiimo's "time agnosia," Evolve's uncredited WHO-5). Cold, alienating, and borrowed.
- **Generic science blurbs** ("proven to reduce anxiety"). The no-idea-underneath tell wearing a lab coat.
- **Coercive button copy** (green "Yes Let's Do It!" vs gray "No") and urgency. The dark-pattern grammar, refused in the open.

**The meta-lesson:** every rival's teaching is a library you must visit, course-shaped content in a tab the struggling user never opens. ALTER's is a just-in-time whisper, one line at the moment the user's own data makes it true. Knowledge decoupled from the moment of need is dead weight.

## PART 4.7. THE LAWS FROM THE 2026-07-10 SESSION (each one was paid for)
The intro-copy marathon (page 2 rewritten ~6 times, two tournaments rejected wholesale) exposed failures the system below did not prevent. These laws now outrank everything under them:

1. **THE VERBATIM-ANCHOR LAW (the big one).** When David says "I like these notions/lines" and quotes source material (Sam Harris, Johnson, anyone), he means the ACTUAL PHRASING. Use his quoted lines verbatim or near-verbatim as the spine; write only the connective tissue. Paraphrasing a loved line into "our own words" is DESTRUCTION, not writing. ("Sam Harris knows how to phrase it correctly.") Bend-don't-free-write already said this; the violation was rewriting the anchors themselves.
2. **ONE DRAFT IN CHAT FIRST.** New copy surface, or David rejected the last attempt → write ONE version in chat and let him critique. NO tournaments, no 10-option fan-outs, until the substance + register + dose are locked through that loop. A tournament amplifies a brief; it cannot fix a wrong one. ("Let's not write a thousand versions right now... you just wrote a bunch of bad things.") Tournaments are for AFTER the brief is proven, when variations on a locked target are wanted — and David asks for the scale.
3. **THE DOSE LAW.** Depth misses in both directions. Too shallow = demystifying comfort-copy with no idea underneath ("meditation is simpler than it sounds"). Too deep = studies + mechanisms dumped pre-practice (the "tech-bro version" overshoot). The right dose for a priming page: page-1-shaped (3-4 short lines), ONE core concept actually EXPLAINED via one concrete instantly-recognizable example (replay an argument and you are back in the argument; the room is gone). Explain a little better, never exhaustively.
4. **STATEFUL COPY.** Every line must match what the user has ACTUALLY DONE at that exact point in the flow. "That was the body" shipped on a page shown BEFORE any breathing. Before writing a page, state what the user has just done and not yet done.
5. **NO FORCED-PICK UI FOR COPY.** Options go in chat or an artifact as readable text David can sit with. The AskUserQuestion picker made him choose without reading ("idk what i chose, didn't get a chance to read"). A pick made blind is not a preference signal — and never treat it as one in COPY-ANCHORS.
6. **JUDGE BLIND SPOTS (append as found).** The Gate-2 judge shares the writer's biases and has now passed: sounds-deep reaches (known), CROSS-SENTENCE switcheroos ("That's not a flaw. That's just..."), and paraphrase-drift from quoted source material. Every new slip becomes (a) a regex in copy-audit.py if mechanical, (b) a named KILLED pattern in COPY-ANCHORS.md if judgment. The switcheroo regex landed 2026-07-10.

## PART 5. THE PROCEDURE — how to write any single line
1. **Locate.** What surface is this, what journey-stage is the user in, what blueprint, what energy? These four are the inputs.
2. **Pick the substance.** Which of the four thesis truths does this line advance? If none, it does not ship.
3. **Pick the teaching move.** Reps-first? Mechanism to name? A mirror-question? Which Voice-Bible craft device carries it (Inversion Seal, Chronicle Cite, Honor-then-Pivot, the Law)?
4. **Bend, do not free-write.** Start from a landed line with the right shape and re-fill it with this substance. Never a blank page.
5. **Adapt.** Shape register and dose to the blueprint, and the mode (promise / teach / cite / chronicle) to the stage.
6. **Gate.** Run `WRITING-SYSTEM` ban list (dash, negation-reversal, slop, template openers) and the anti-template test, then `VOICE-BIBLE` lint (register, "I" at thresholds only, data-beats-adjectives), then the 20 percent cut. Read it aloud for metronome rhythm.
7. **Spoken vs screen.** Spoken lines are hash-keyed contracts (retire-first, re-record in batches). Spend the cleverness budget on screen, which is free.

### Fully worked example
Surface: the landing after the day-one first rep. Stage: **Arrival**. Blueprint: unknown yet (default, skeptic-safe, warm). Energy: unread.
- Substance: truth 2 (the catch / ownership) + truth 3 (first evidence).
- Teaching move: show, do not declare; ownership after the rep.
- Device: the Forward Close is wrong here (this is reflective), use ownership + a Chronicle Cite seeded for the future.
- Bend from the landed ownership shape.
- Draft: "Feel that? However small, that shift was yours. You made it. And I wrote it down. Your first piece of evidence for who you are becoming."
- Gate: no dash, no negation-reversal, no slop, not copy-pasteable to another app (it references the record mechanic), reads uneven when spoken. Ships.

## THE AUDIT — the two automatic gates (this is why the docs alone kept failing)
The writing docs failed for one reason: the author graded his own work, and an author passes his own slop. Every real gate David trusts (the code ratchet, balance's AI-tell judge weighted above the persona panel) works because it is INDEPENDENT and ADVERSARIAL. So copy gets two gates it cannot self-approve past, run BEFORE David ever sees a line.

**Gate 1, deterministic (`_dev/copy-audit.py`).** Regex catches the mechanical tells with zero judgment: dashes, emoji, the negation-contrast cadence, slop and hype words, template openers, the wrap-up bow, coercive CTA grammar, and the "no idea underneath" family (grand-abstract superlatives, vague quantifiers, clinical jargon). Run it on any candidate line or a file of lines. It exits nonzero on a hard tell so it can gate.
`python3 _dev/copy-audit.py "the line"` or `--file lines.txt`

**Gate 2, adversarial judge (an independent agent) — SCORED AGAINST DAVID'S RATED ANCHORS, not just a rubric.** The regex cannot catch vague cheerleading with no mechanical tell, and an abstract rubric fails a subtler way: an LLM judge SHARES the writer's biases and rewards "sounds-good" writing David's ear rejects (it called the KILLED fridge-analogy line "the strongest line"). So the judge MUST first read `_specs/COPY-ANCHORS.md` — David's real KILLED lines (each with its named failure pattern: forced/decorative analogy, withholding tease, flourish-ending, essay/stiff cadence, meditation-madness, vague-pronoun, generic-category beat, shallow, cheesy) and his EPIC lines — and score every candidate BY COMPARISON: which KILLED pattern does it smell like, which EPIC anchor is it closest to. Rubric-clean is not enough; if it resembles a KILLED anchor, KILL it. It is prompted to refute, never approve; route to a cheap model. **Every line David rejects gets appended to COPY-ANCHORS.md as a KILLED anchor with the pattern; every line he loves becomes EPIC. The bank is how the judge learns his taste instead of the model's.**

**The hard rule:** no line reaches David until it has passed Gate 1 clean and survived Gate 2. The author never self-approves. When a new failure slips through anyway, its pattern is added to `copy-audit.py` (Gate 1) or the kill-shot list (Gate 2) so the same slop can never pass twice.
