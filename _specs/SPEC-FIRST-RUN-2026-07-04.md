## §0 THE LAWS OF THIS SPEC

1. **STATE FIRST, STORY SECOND (Tony's law).** Never teach into a flat nervous system. Every ceremony opens by raising state; content lands into the raised state.
2. **LITURGY OVER EVENT.** Every day has the same skeleton: **OPEN → AIM → LIVE → LESSON → CLOSE.** Day 1 is not special content — it is the liturgy performed for the first time. This kills the day-2 cliff structurally.
3. **MEANING IS ENACTED, NEVER ANNOUNCED (the register law).** The interaction carries the metaphor; words stay sober. Mythic lines ≤ 12 words. No line may admire the app's own role ("who I work for" = the banned register). The onboarding Spark script is the bar. If a line explains its own metaphor, cut one of the two.
4. **THE GATE LOOP** under every ceremony: coherence (10s orb) → honest expectation line → threshold crossing → content → seal variant → reincorporation (the world visibly changed) → delta when relevant. Evidence feeds next expectation; the constant sensory signature conditions faster gates.
5. **ONE METHOD: THE STACK.** posture → breath (10s orb) → hand-on-heart + one re-felt appreciation → the word (spoken or breathed) → **the anchor** (thumb-to-forefinger mudra, fired at peak). 90s full · 20s floor. It opens every session, stands alone as the panic button, and conditions the anchor for portable firing.
6. **ONE EVIDENCE LANGUAGE.** The atomic unit is **the mark** (a real thing done). All scores are lenses over marks. v1 scope: one visual stamp (+N gold) everywhere; full mark-ledger unification = LATER.
7. **UI never says magic / spell / ritual / hypnosis** (standing law, 2026-07-01). Crowley-form, science-content.
8. All existing constitution + depth laws stand (no typing ever · no caps · reward-never-shame · one nudge/day · B4 EN+RU same commit · no emoji · solid pieces).

## §4 THE RANGE (the flagship new build)

**Scene:** you at a line; a field recedes into haze. **Targets = your actual goals**, placed by you at the distance they live at: close = this week · mid = this season · far = this year · horizon = someday. Far targets small, misty, always visible.
**The rule (all the meaning):** *"You only ever get one arrow — today's."* Aim at the NEAREST target → choose the arrowhead (baby-step chips: "15 minutes on it" / "the first tiny piece" / "just open it" / suggestions from P.starter) → loose → the arrow lands **and becomes a real block planted on today's timeline** (safe path: blocks().push + reflow, prio 2, domain from the goal). Ceremony exits into the planner.
**The living system:** one arrow per morning (AIM beat). Arrows stick where they land — a scatter, never a score (*"arrows are data, not grades"*). Near targets fill and are carried off the field (goal-done ceremony). Far targets drift closer as feeders complete (the domino, visible). Ignored targets wait in the mist — no rot, no red; at ≥2 weeks the guardian may ask ONE honest mirror about it. WOOP rides after the first arrow: *"what knocks arrows down?"* → wind named → plan chip (writes goal.woop).
**Floor dose (mom):** one near target only — *"one thing you'd like more of this week?"*; distance/horizon unlocks by living. Ceiling: up to 3 targets day-1, arrows/day may rise to 2-3 by door.
**State:** `S.range = { targets:[{id,title,domain,horizon:"week"|"season"|"year"|"someday",placedK,arrows:[{k,dx,dy,blockId,done}],woop,retiredK}], lastArrowK }`. SCHEMA 4→5 + migration (existing `S.goals` entries import as targets, horizon guessed "season").

## §5 THE RELIGHT + STORM MODE (failure choreography)

**The Relight** — ONE ceremony, always identical, for every return after a gap ≥2 days or a broken pact window: the Hearth, fire low but alive (*a pact fire cannot die — only sleep*); one breath; one mirror — *"What happened?"* (tired / life happened / lost the thread / don't want to say — ALL accepted, zero penalty, saves gapReason); strike → blow → the fire returns; one arrow re-aimed at the nearest target. 60 seconds. Replaces the current comeback-ladder card as the primary return path (the ladder remains the lightweight same-day version). Identical every time — familiarity is the mercy.
**Storm mode:** a door in Settings + offered once by the guardian if gauge ≤1 for 3 consecutive days: collapses the app to Well + Hearth + plain tracking. No path, no lessons, no arrows required, until the user leaves it. Copy: *"We hold the fort. Nothing else."*
