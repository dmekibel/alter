## 0. CRITIQUE OF THE v1 DAVID SIMULATION (what was wrong)

1. **Work-monoculture.** Day 1 was deep-block-shaped. The Big 3 is Energy × Work × LOVE — v1 simulated one third. Real days contain a messy room, laundry, food, sunlight, people, the sea. The planner must make the day BREATHE across domains, and the app must treat "clean the room" with the same intelligence as "deep work."
2. **The toolbox never appeared.** Meditation, breathwork, micro-stack, reprogramming toolkit — the entire retreat-half of the app was absent from the sim. Tools must appear IN the day, placed by time and trigger, not listed.
3. **The evening was underweighted.** 20-second PM close is right as a floor — but the pre-sleep window is the subconscious-plasticity window (Maltz's theater of the mind, Dispenza's pre-sleep rehearsal). The PM close is where reprogramming lives. v1 missed the app's whole "guru" organ.
4. **No goals intake.** "How it takes my goals" — v1 never asked David what he's building. Course: Targets / 3 Quests / WOOP. Needs a designed moment (day 2-3, not onboarding).
5. **Recovery undesigned.** No beach, no off-switch. Course: Carpe Rhythms ON/OFF — recovery is a design choice. The app must PLAN wideness, not just permit it.
6. **No spaced repetition.** Theory cards taught once = forgotten. The course's spaced-repetition spine needs an organ — the unbuilt brain-gym spec is exactly this (weekly micro-review deck).
7. **Environment never asked.** The room/base state is a live psychological variable (environment design beats willpower — Module V) and v1 never asked about it.
8. **The four voices weren't named.** Retreat × coach × therapist × drill-sergeant — v1 had coach + a bit of sergeant. The BALANCE is the product. Mapping: MORNING = sergeant+retreat (short, embodied, primed) · DAY = coach (plans, sprints, triggers) · EVENING = therapist+guru (reflection, reframe, reprogram). The register dial (Softly/Straight/Push-me) modulates sergeant amplitude only — the therapist and retreat never get louder or quieter with it.

## 4. WHAT THE PERSONAS TEACH THE SYSTEM (cross-learnings)

1. The SAME organs serve everyone; only routing/dose/flavor change. No persona forks — parameters.
2. The chore-sprint + chore-chain is core infrastructure (mom's work domain, brother's mastery quest, David's room) — build once, three uses.
3. The drift dial needs a per-user DEFAULT route (efficacy/bored/energy) learned from profile, refined by outcomes.
4. The PM close is universally the most valuable 60 seconds: floor = 2 taps; ceiling = taps + WOOP + mantra beat.
5. Sergeant amplitude = register dial; therapist and retreat are register-invariant.
6. Time-symmetry (floating morning) matters beyond the brother — anyone's day starts when it starts.
