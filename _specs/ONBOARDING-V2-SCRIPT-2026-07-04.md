## 0. THE SHAPE (what changed from v881)

**The old sin:** the onboarding *told* the thesis ("every real thing you do, I remember") before proving it. V2 deletes the pitch beat entirely — the thesis is **demonstrated** in beat 2–3 (you breathe; the app writes it into the record in front of you) and only *named* after it already happened. Show → then say.

**Seven beats, ~75 seconds:**

| # | Beat | Ask or gift? | Data captured |
|---|------|-------------|---------------|
| 0 | The Meeting | gift (presence) | openBand (silent, from the clock) |
| 1 | The Truth | ask (2 sec, 1 tap) | vibe → appetite seed |
| 2 | The Breath | gift (done *with* you) | — |
| 3 | The Write | gift (the payoff + the thesis, proven) | — |
| 4 | The Wall | ask (1 tap, skippable) | block shape → Doorway promise |
| 5 | The Pact | ask (a 1.2s hold — the only "costly" ask, placed at the peak: right after the gift) | pactAt → honesty-callback |
| 6 | The Card + The Seed | gift (identity + world-born) | — → hands off to the routed Day 1 |

Invariant: **gift before every ask, ask at the desire spike, answer used within minutes.** No beat collects what the day won't spend.

2. **The opt-out is a styled equal option inside the grid** («Ничего из этого» / «Не важно») — never a skip link.
