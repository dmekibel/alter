## 0. THE SHAPE (what changed from v881)

**The old sin:** the onboarding *told* the thesis ("every real thing you do, I remember") before proving it. V2 deletes the pitch beat entirely — the thesis is **demonstrated** in beat 2–3 (you breathe; the app writes it into the record in front of you) and only *named* after it already happened. Show → then say.

**Seven beats, ~75 seconds:**

| # | Beat | Ask or gift? | Data captured |
|---|------|-------------|---------------|
| 0 | The Meeting | gift (presence) | openBand (silent, from the clock) |
| 1 | The Truth | ask (2 sec, 1 tap) | vibe → appetite seed |
| 2 | The Breath | gift (done *with* you) | — |
| 3 | The Write | gift (the payoff + the thesis, proven) | — |
| 4 | The Wall | ask (1 tap, skippable) | block shape → Doorway promise |
| 5 | The Pact | ask (a 1.2s hold — the only "costly" ask, placed at the peak: right after the gift) | pactAt → honesty-callback |
| 6 | The Card + The Seed | gift (identity + world-born) | — → hands off to the routed Day 1 |

Invariant: **gift before every ask, ask at the desire spike, answer used within minutes.** No beat collects what the day won't spend.

## 2. THE ROUTING MATRIX (onboarding → which Day 1 you get)

Two axes decide everything (both already captured, zero extra questions):

| | **open before 09** | **09–17** | **17–22** | **after 22** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **thriving** | A. FULL DAY | A. FULL DAY | A′. EVENING SPRINT | C′. NIGHT SEED |
| **coasting** | B. LIGHT DAY | B. LIGHT DAY | B′. ONE-THING EVENING | C′. NIGHT SEED |
| **stuck** | B. LIGHT DAY (+doorway tease) | B. LIGHT DAY (+doorway tease) | B′. ONE-THING EVENING | C′. NIGHT SEED |
| **overwhelmed** | C. RELIEF DAY | C. RELIEF DAY | C. RELIEF DAY | C′. NIGHT SEED |

- **A. FULL DAY** = Five Stones as designed (DO → TELL → PROPOSE×3 → SEE → CLOSE).
- **B. LIGHT DAY** = Five Stones with PROPOSE capped at 1–2 + softer copy; stuck adds the Doorway *tease* («завтра — тот самый край»).
- **C. RELIEF DAY** = the gauge-routed RELIEF door replaces stones 1–3: 5-min micro-stack (tracked visibly: «я это записываю — всё настоящее считается») → «ещё пять?» → SEE → CLOSE-as-conversation. *A depleted user is never asked to be productive.*
- **C′. NIGHT SEED** = if the first open is near midnight, Day 1 is 3 minutes long by design: the seed, one line («Сегодня уже случилось. Начнём с утра — я разбужу день, не тебя.»), CLOSE-lite, and Day 1 *officially* becomes tomorrow. No stones fire at 23:40.
- **Blocker shape** modifies none of the routes — it arms exactly one node (the Doorway) on days 2–7. One answer, one artifact.

4. **21:50 CLOSE-lite**: one line, conversation register. Finale: «День 1: ты пришла на нуле и всё равно дала дню пять настоящих минут. Это и есть навык.»

   ✅ never asked to be productive; the tracker taught itself by being watched.

### Persona C — «брат» (coasting, opens 13:00, blocker: none) — *minimal ceremony, skip-prone*

   ✅ skip-path lands somewhere real; zero punishment for minimalism.

### Persona D — «мама» (stuck, RU, opens 10:30, blocker: fear) — *gentle, every string RU*

4. **Day 2 Doorway** (the whole reason the blocker question exists): «Помнишь стену? Вот её край: [5 минут, только посмотреть]. Выход рядом.» Taken or not — the node retires after one take; if declined twice it goes quiet (no nagging a wound).
