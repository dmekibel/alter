# Self-help stack content — transcripts + mantra (David's sources)

## 4 guided-meditation styles (= David's full 4-in-1 hybrid teachers) — ALL TRANSCRIBED via tactiq
1. **style1-blackstone-fundamental-consciousness.txt** — Judith Blackstone (https://youtu.be/5i8Flx_KBTQ). Embodiment: inhabit each body part → "attune to the quality of [self/love/power/voice] inside it" → fine upward energy thread → dissolve into one continuous space ("you ARE that space"). The love/heart + awakening layer.
2. **style2-sam-harris-mindfulness.txt** — Sam Harris (https://youtu.be/tw7XBKhZJh4). Anchor on breath → merely witness all objects of consciousness arising/changing/passing. Thoughts aren't failure.
3. **style3-headspace-andy-reset.txt** — Headspace/Andy (https://youtu.be/SHC1ZiUu-9E). Warm permission · "no need to change anything, just noticing" · count 1(in)/2(out) on the out-breath · "appreciate the sense of space" · brief "let the mind be completely free" before returning.
4. **style4-adyashanti-resting-as-awareness.txt** — Adyashanti (https://youtu.be/hIX_zk5NN6g). Nondual capstone: there's nothing to DO — relinquish the "doer" and the need to understand ("pat it on the head, it's irrelevant"); rest as awareness (being, not doing); name the seductive mind ("just understand this one last thing…" — don't take the bait); consistency = not being lured back into figuring-out; "you and awareness are not two."
