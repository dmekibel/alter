PowerUp's planning model: a long-term goal decomposes into mid-term milestones, then scheduled weekly and monthly steps, then daily activities in the day calendar — shown with a knee-surgery example, across life domains like health, finance, and work.

Long-term goal months → years
e.g. “get my knee fixed” · “stay on budget this year”
Mid-term subplans / milestones weeks
see doctor → MRI → pre-op → surgery → rehab
This week / month — app picks WHEN days
call doctor Mon · book MRI Wed · the app recommends the dates
Daily activities → your day-calendar today
the dated bubbles you actually do — and track
