The expanded Six Domains taxonomy with eight categories plus drift, listing many activities in each, and a note on activities that change domain by intent.

MOVE
walk · gym · run · stretch · yoga · sport · bike · hike · dance
NOURISH
breakfast · lunch · dinner · cook · snack · coffee · water · groceries
FOCUS
deep work · study · admin · email · meetings · planning · code
CREATE
paint · draw · music · write · YouTube · design · build · practice a skill
CONNECT
family · friends · call · date · hang out · help someone · community
PLAY
games · TV · movies · fun reading · hobbies · explore · mess around
RESTORE
sleep · nap · meditate · breathe · bath · nature · journal · pray · just rest
UPKEEP
shower · grooming · clean room · laundry · dishes · errands · money · doctor
DRIFT
unconscious scroll · zone-out · mindless snack — the only “off-plan”
Same activity, different domain by INTENT
Reading → to learn = Focus · for fun = Play · reflective = Restore
Walking → exercise = Move · contemplative = Restore
Journaling → reflective = Restore · creative writing = Create
Cooking → fuel = Nourish · creative hobby = Create
Scrolling → conscious & chosen = Play · unconscious = Drift
